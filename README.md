# Heat Exchanger Heat Transfer Calculator

## Aim
To calculate the rate of heat transfer in a heat exchanger using the overall heat transfer equation.

## Objective
This program determines the heat transfer rate between two fluids in a heat exchanger based on given parameters.

## Theory
Heat exchangers are widely used in chemical industries to transfer heat between two fluids without mixing them.

The heat transfer rate is calculated using the equation:

Q = U × A × ΔT

Where:

- Q = Heat transfer rate (Watts)
- U = Overall heat transfer coefficient (W/m²·K)
- A = Heat transfer area (m²)
- ΔT = Temperature difference between fluids (K)

This simplified relation helps estimate the performance of heat exchanger systems.
